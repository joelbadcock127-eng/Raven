A Secluded Tasmanian Sanctuary for the Discerning Traveller

More than a getaway, Ten Fifty Bakers is a private wilderness retreat in North West Tasmania designed for those who seek beauty that feels intimate, not staged. Luxury here is silence, space, and clean air. It’s walking barefoot to your bath under the stars, knowing you’re the only one for miles.

Your Stay, Your Pace.

Every element of Ten Fifty Bakers is crafted for presence, from the scent of warm timber as you step inside, to the golden light spilling across stone at dusk. Whether you spend your days hiking through our private trails, the adjoining Narawntapu National Park or reading by the fire, this is a place to recalibrate and savour the kind of time you can’t find anywhere else.
